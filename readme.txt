This plugin enables you to display your Vimeo videos in an albums-grouped video gallery.
 
== Description ==
 
This plugin allows you to display your Vimeo videos on your website grouped by however your albums are organized within your Vimeo account. You can control the look and feel of your videos and albums by editing the settings within your Vimeo account. Learn more at http://www.basebuildguys.com/blog/how-to-create-an-online-video-library-with-vimeo-not-youtube.
 
= Special Features =
* Display a grid of albums that contain your Vimeo hosted videos.

* Use Vimeo settings to control the look and feel.
* Upgrade to display infinite albums.

* Upgrade to add a search functionality.
* Upgrade to feature recent videos at the top.
